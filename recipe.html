Jollof Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 tomatoes (blended)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chicken or beef stock
  • Vegetable oil
  • Optional: bell pepper, carrot, green peas

Preparation Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions until translucent.
  2. Add blended tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook for 10–15 minutes until it thickens.
  3. Add curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt. Stir well.
  4. Pour in the stock and bring to a boil.
  5. Add washed rice into the mixture and stir.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is done and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Optionally add vegetables and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with meat, fish, or salad.
